How to Replace a Dryer Belt

Best for: motor runs but drum doesn’t spin, burning rubber smell, thumping.

Safety: Unplug the dryer before opening panels. If it’s gas, turn off gas supply too.

Tools & parts

  • Replacement belt (match model)
  • Phillips screwdriver / nut driver (varies)
  • Work gloves

Step-by-step (general)

  1. Unplug the dryer.
  2. Access the drum: remove lint filter, then remove top/front panel screws (varies).
  3. Release belt tension: push idler pulley to loosen belt.
  4. Remove drum carefully (take a photo of belt routing first).
  5. Install new belt around drum with ribbed side against drum.
  6. Re-route belt through idler pulley and motor pulley.
  7. Spin drum by hand one rotation to confirm tracking.
  8. Reassemble panels and screws.
  9. Test a short timed dry.
