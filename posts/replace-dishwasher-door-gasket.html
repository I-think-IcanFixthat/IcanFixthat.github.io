How to Replace a Dishwasher Door Gasket

Best for: water leaking from the sides/top of the door during wash.

Tip: If leaks happen only at the bottom, it may be a door sweep or spray-arm issue—not the side gasket.

Tools & parts

  • Correct gasket for your model
  • Mild cleaner + rag
  • Scissors (sometimes)

Step-by-step

  1. Open door and locate gasket seated in the channel around the tub opening.
  2. Pull old gasket out slowly from one corner.
  3. Clean the channel so the new gasket seats fully.
  4. Press new gasket in starting at top center, then work down both sides evenly.
  5. Do not stretch the gasket—press it in naturally as you go.
  6. Close door and let it sit 30–60 minutes.
  7. Test a quick cycle and check for leaks.
